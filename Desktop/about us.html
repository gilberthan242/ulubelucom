ABOUT US

The root of the company started as early as 1940s by a hard working individual who lived in Talang Padang village, 75 Km away from the main city of Lampung, Indonesia. He bought and sold tiny quantity of coffee at its surroundings, including Ulubelu Mountainous Range area. Due to strong alliances with the local farmers, the healthy relationships soon bloomed and PT. Ulubelu Cofco Abadi was established.

In early 2010, the third generation of the family moved the headquarter to the heart of Lampung province, right in the industrial center of Bandar Lampung, and about 5 Km from Panjang International Sea Port. In order to strengthen Ulubelu brand in overseas, PT. Ulubelu Spesialti Kopi was established in 2018.

We hold pride in our professionalism and in the integrity we have had for decades in operating in the industry. We utilize the modern technology while maintaining the traditional method when applicable. In addition to being dedicated to quality, we are also devoted to preserving the environment, as well as maintaining the flourished relationships with customers, suppliers, and workers. We realize our powerful potential to utilize our resources, and we contend to be the best supplier for coffee of your choice.

To prove our commitment, we are registered as member of Association of Indonesian Coffee Exporters. We hold Food and Drug Administration Certificate, Certificate by Indonesia Trade Ministry, and Halal Certificate. We also have in-house R-Grader to ensuere cupping quality.
